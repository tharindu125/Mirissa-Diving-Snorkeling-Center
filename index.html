PADI Courses

PADI Courses – Become a Certified Diver in Mirissa

Train with professional PADI-certified instructors in the beautiful waters of Mirissa and earn your globally recognized diving license. Whether you're a beginner or looking to advance, our safe, high-quality courses offer the perfect setting to build your diving skills.

Available Courses :
✅ PADI Open Water Diver License

🔹 Price: $400 per person

Perfect for beginners, this course includes theory, pool sessions, and open water dives. After completion, you’ll be certified to dive up to 18 meters anywhere in the world.

✅ PADI Advanced License

🔹 Price: $400 per person

This course is for certified divers who want to go deeper and improve their skills. Learn navigation, deep diving, and more while exploring Mirissa’s rich marine life.

Get certified in paradise become a PADI Diver with us in Mirissa!
